Waiata 02: Cave of Treasures

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
te ana i te whenua, te pōuri loko,
Ārama a me Īwa, te tūmanako hoko,
Gula, iniseni, mira mau,
Atua haʻawi mai, te aroha hau.

[Verse 2]
te mārama iki, te ahi mehana,
te wai ola, te ora banana,
te kupu taurangi paʻa i te ana nei,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te whakapono lei.

[Chorus]
te ana waiwai, hale a me hale paʻahao,
te pōuri pōhaku, Atua ao,
te tūmanako ola mau, te mārama e hele mai,
te Kaiwhakaora kokoke, te aroha nui mai!

[Verse 3]
Ārama uē, Īwa kaumaha,
te hara mamao, Atua aha,
te anahera kōkua, te kupu taurangi mau,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te rangi hau.

[Verse 4]
Waiwai ʻekolu no ka lā,
Gula, iniseni, mira kā,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai nei,
Atua keiki, te tūmanako lei.

[Chorus]
te ana waiwai, hale a me hale paʻahao,
te pōuri pōhaku, Atua ao,
te tūmanako ola mau, te mārama e hele mai,
te Kaiwhakaora kokoke, te aroha nui mai!

[Bridge]
te ahi māhana i te ana nei,
te wai ola, te ora lei,
Atua mau, te kupu taurangi paʻa,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te tūmanako kā.

[Outro]
I loko o te ana... kali ana... te whakaoranga...
