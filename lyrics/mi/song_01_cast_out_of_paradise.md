Waiata 01: Cast Out of Paradise

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
te māra Pararaiha, Atua i hana,
Ārama i te whenua mai, te mārama hoʻomaka,
Īwa i kona ʻaoʻao, te aroha pū,
te rangi a me te whenua, nani pau.

[Verse 2]
te nakahi hele mai, te pōuri lawe,
te hara komo mai, te mate haʻalele,
te māra nalowale, te tūmanako make,
Atua hoʻouna mai, te whakaoranga ake.

[Chorus]
Pararaiha nalowale, te rangi mamao,
te mārama pio, te pōuri ao,
Atua aloha mau, te kupu taurangi ola,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te tūmanako hola!

[Verse 3]
Ārama a me Īwa, te whenua hele,
te ana i loko, te ahi mehana,
te anahera kiaʻi, te rākau mamao,
te whakapono paʻa mau, te aroha ao.

[Verse 4]
te hara nui, te mate kokoke,
Atua nui aʻe, te ora loke,
te kupu taurangi paʻa, te whakaoranga mau,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te rangi hau.

[Chorus]
Pararaiha nalowale, te rangi mamao,
te mārama pio, te pōuri ao,
Atua aloha mau, te kupu taurangi ola,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te tūmanako hola!

[Bridge]
te wai kahe, te ahi ʻā,
te rākau ulu, te ora pā,
Atua lohe, te aroha aloha,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te tūmanako ola.

[Outro]
Pararaiha e hoʻi mai... Atua mau...
