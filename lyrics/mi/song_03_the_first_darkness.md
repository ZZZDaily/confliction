Waiata 03: The First Darkness

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
te pōuri mua, te mārama nalowale,
Ārama a me Īwa, makaʻu hale,
te rangi mamao, te whenua koʻikoʻi,
Atua lohe, te tūmanako poi.

[Verse 2]
te ahi iki, te wai ola,
te whakapono paʻa, te aroha hola,
te pōuri nui, te mārama e hele mai,
Atua aloha, te kupu taurangi mai.

[Chorus]
te pōuri mua, makaʻu a me kaumaha,
te mārama nalowale, te rangi aha,
Atua mau, te tūmanako ola,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te ora hola!

[Verse 3]
te nakahi holo, te hara komo,
te mate kokoke, te ora homo,
Atua nui, te whakaoranga mau,
te kupu taurangi paʻa, te whakapono hau.

[Verse 4]
te anahera e hele mai, te mārama lawe,
te pōuri holo, te tūmanako hawe,
te māra memory, Pararaiha moe,
Atua mau, te aroha noe.

[Chorus]
te pōuri mua, makaʻu a me kaumaha,
te mārama nalowale, te rangi aha,
Atua mau, te tūmanako ola,
te Kaiwhakaora e hele mai, te ora hola!

[Bridge]
te ahi ʻā i ka pō,
te wai kahe, te ora kō,
Atua kiaʻi, te anahera mau,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te tūmanako hau.

[Outro]
te pōuri pau... te mārama e hele mai...
