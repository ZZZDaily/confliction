Waiata 20: The Garden Remembered

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
te māra nani, Pararaiha moe,
Atua hana, te mārama poe,
Ārama hele, Īwa pū,
te rangi kokoke, te whenua hū.

[Verse 2]
te nakahi hele mai, te hara lawe,
te mate kokoke, Atua hawe,
te whakaoranga hele mai, te tūmanako ola,
te whakapono paʻa, te Kaiwhakaora hola.

[Chorus]
Pararaiha e hoʻi mai, te tūmanako ola,
te māra nani, Atua hola,
te mārama mālamalama, te pōuri pau,
te whakaoranga mau, te aroha hau!

[Verse 3]
te ana pōhaku, te ahi mehana,
te wai ola, te ora banana,
te anahera kiaʻi, Atua mau,
te kupu taurangi paʻa, te aroha hau.

[Verse 4]
Ārama uē, Īwa kaumaha,
Atua lohe, te kupu taurangi aha,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te Kaiwhakaora mai,
Pararaiha hoʻi, te tūmanako kai.

[Chorus]
Pararaiha e hoʻi mai, te tūmanako ola,
te māra nani, Atua hola,
te mārama mālamalama, te pōuri pau,
te whakaoranga mau, te aroha hau!

[Bridge]
te mate pau, te ora ola,
te pōuri holo, te mārama hola,
Atua mau, te aroha aloha,
te whakaoranga kokoke, te rangi ola.

[Outro]
Pararaiha e hoʻi mai... Atua mau...
