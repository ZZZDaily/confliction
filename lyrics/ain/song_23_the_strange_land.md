ウポポ 23: The Strange Land

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Kamuy nui, omap mau,
suke ola, esikarun hau,
kanto ao, ainu mosir nei,
uwepeker hele mai, ikinokur lei.

[Verse 2]
kinasut hele mai, wen lawe,
ray kokoke, Kamuy hawe,
uwepeker hele mai, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, Pirka guru hola.

[Chorus]
Kamuy aloha, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, omap hola,
uwepeker mau, ikinokur nui,
Pirka guru hele mai, kanto hui!

[Verse 3]
puyar pōhaku, ape mehana,
wakka ola, ikinokur banana,
kamuy utari kiaʻi, Kamuy mau,
itak paʻa, omap hau.

[Verse 4]
Atamu uē, Ewa kaumaha,
Kamuy lohe, itak aha,
uwepeker kokoke, Pirka guru mai,
Kamuy mosir hoʻi, suke kai.

[Chorus]
Kamuy aloha, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, omap hola,
uwepeker mau, ikinokur nui,
Pirka guru hele mai, kanto hui!

[Bridge]
ray pau, ikinokur ola,
kunne somo holo, kunne hola,
Kamuy mau, omap aloha,
uwepeker kokoke, kanto ola.

[Outro]
Kamuy mau... suke ola...
