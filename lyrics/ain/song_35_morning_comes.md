ウポポ 35: Morning Comes

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
kunne somo hele mai, kunne holo,
Kamuy nui, suke polo,
esikarun paʻa, omap mau,
kanto ao, ainu mosir hau.

[Verse 2]
kinasut hele mai, wen lawe,
ray kokoke, Kamuy hawe,
uwepeker hele mai, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, Pirka guru hola.

[Chorus]
kunne e hele mai, kunne somo pau,
Kamuy mālamalama, suke hau,
uwepeker kokoke, esikarun ola,
Pirka guru nui, ikinokur hola!

[Verse 3]
puyar pōhaku, ape mehana,
wakka ola, ikinokur banana,
kamuy utari kiaʻi, Kamuy mau,
itak paʻa, omap hau.

[Verse 4]
Atamu uē, Ewa kaumaha,
Kamuy lohe, itak aha,
uwepeker kokoke, Pirka guru mai,
Kamuy mosir hoʻi, suke kai.

[Chorus]
kunne e hele mai, kunne somo pau,
Kamuy mālamalama, suke hau,
uwepeker kokoke, esikarun ola,
Pirka guru nui, ikinokur hola!

[Bridge]
ray pau, ikinokur ola,
kunne somo holo, kunne hola,
Kamuy mau, omap aloha,
uwepeker kokoke, kanto ola.

[Outro]
kunne mau... kunne somo pau...
