ウポポ 51: Fight of Faith

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
esikarun paʻa, suke ola,
Kamuy nui, omap hola,
uwepeker kokoke, ikinokur mau,
Pirka guru hele mai, kanto hau.

[Verse 2]
kinasut hele mai, wen lawe,
ray kokoke, Kamuy hawe,
uwepeker hele mai, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, Pirka guru hola.

[Chorus]
esikarun lanakila, suke ao,
Kamuy mau, omap hao,
uwepeker ola, ikinokur nui,
Pirka guru kokoke, kanto hui!

[Verse 3]
puyar pōhaku, ape mehana,
wakka ola, ikinokur banana,
kamuy utari kiaʻi, Kamuy mau,
itak paʻa, omap hau.

[Verse 4]
Atamu uē, Ewa kaumaha,
Kamuy lohe, itak aha,
uwepeker kokoke, Pirka guru mai,
Kamuy mosir hoʻi, suke kai.

[Chorus]
esikarun lanakila, suke ao,
Kamuy mau, omap hao,
uwepeker ola, ikinokur nui,
Pirka guru kokoke, kanto hui!

[Bridge]
ray pau, ikinokur ola,
kunne somo holo, kunne hola,
Kamuy mau, omap aloha,
uwepeker kokoke, kanto ola.

[Outro]
esikarun mau... suke ola...
