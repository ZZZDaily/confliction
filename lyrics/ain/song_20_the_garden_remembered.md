ウポポ 20: The Garden Remembered

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
paye nani, Kamuy mosir moe,
Kamuy hana, kunne poe,
Atamu hele, Ewa pū,
kanto kokoke, ainu mosir hū.

[Verse 2]
kinasut hele mai, wen lawe,
ray kokoke, Kamuy hawe,
uwepeker hele mai, suke ola,
esikarun paʻa, Pirka guru hola.

[Chorus]
Kamuy mosir e hoʻi mai, suke ola,
paye nani, Kamuy hola,
kunne mālamalama, kunne somo pau,
uwepeker mau, omap hau!

[Verse 3]
puyar pōhaku, ape mehana,
wakka ola, ikinokur banana,
kamuy utari kiaʻi, Kamuy mau,
itak paʻa, omap hau.

[Verse 4]
Atamu uē, Ewa kaumaha,
Kamuy lohe, itak aha,
uwepeker kokoke, Pirka guru mai,
Kamuy mosir hoʻi, suke kai.

[Chorus]
Kamuy mosir e hoʻi mai, suke ola,
paye nani, Kamuy hola,
kunne mālamalama, kunne somo pau,
uwepeker mau, omap hau!

[Bridge]
ray pau, ikinokur ola,
kunne somo holo, kunne hola,
Kamuy mau, omap aloha,
uwepeker kokoke, kanto ola.

[Outro]
Kamuy mosir e hoʻi mai... Kamuy mau...
