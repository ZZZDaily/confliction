Song 48: The Valley of Death

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
death awalau, life hope,
God kiaʻi, faith rope,
light mālamalama, darkness ao,
hope ola, salvation hao.

[Verse 2]
di snake hele mai, di sin lawe,
death kokoke, God hawe,
salvation hele mai, hope ola,
faith paʻa, di Savior hola.

[Chorus]
death pau, life lanakila,
God nui, di Savior ila,
hope mau, faith ola,
salvation kokoke, love hola!

[Verse 3]
di cave pōhaku, fire mehana,
water ola, life banana,
di angel kiaʻi, God mau,
di promise paʻa, love hau.

[Verse 4]
Adam uē, Eve kaumaha,
God lohe, di promise aha,
salvation kokoke, di Savior mai,
Paradise hoʻi, hope kai.

[Chorus]
death pau, life lanakila,
God nui, di Savior ila,
hope mau, faith ola,
salvation kokoke, love hola!

[Bridge]
death pau, life ola,
darkness holo, light hola,
God mau, love aloha,
salvation kokoke, heaven ola.

[Outro]
death pau... life lanakila...
