Meneshinana 42: Paradise Restored

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
tʃakra nani, Paraíso moe,
Dios hana, aʧana poe,
Adán hele, Eva pū,
hanan paʧa kokoke, allpa hū.

[Verse 2]
amaru hele mai, uʃpela lawe,
wañuy kokoke, Dios hawe,
kawsana hele mai, suyay ola,
iñiy paʻa, Qespichiq hola.

[Chorus]
Paraíso e hoʻi mai, suyay ola,
tʃakra nani, Dios hola,
aʧana mālamalama, tuta pau,
kawsana mau, kuyay hau!

[Verse 3]
maʧay pōhaku, nina mehana,
yaku ola, kawsay banana,
ángel kiaʻi, Dios mau,
ñisqa paʻa, kuyay hau.

[Verse 4]
Adán uē, Eva kaumaha,
Dios lohe, ñisqa aha,
kawsana kokoke, Qespichiq mai,
Paraíso hoʻi, suyay kai.

[Chorus]
Paraíso e hoʻi mai, suyay ola,
tʃakra nani, Dios hola,
aʧana mālamalama, tuta pau,
kawsana mau, kuyay hau!

[Bridge]
wañuy pau, kawsay ola,
tuta holo, aʧana hola,
Dios mau, kuyay aloha,
kawsana kokoke, hanan paʧa ola.

[Outro]
Paraíso e hoʻi mai... Dios mau...
