Meneshinana 33: Seth the Replacement

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
iñiy paʻa, suyay ola,
Dios nui, kuyay hola,
kawsana kokoke, kawsay mau,
Qespichiq hele mai, hanan paʧa hau.

[Verse 2]
amaru hele mai, uʃpela lawe,
wañuy kokoke, Dios hawe,
kawsana hele mai, suyay ola,
iñiy paʻa, Qespichiq hola.

[Chorus]
iñiy lanakila, suyay ao,
Dios mau, kuyay hao,
kawsana ola, kawsay nui,
Qespichiq kokoke, hanan paʧa hui!

[Verse 3]
maʧay pōhaku, nina mehana,
yaku ola, kawsay banana,
ángel kiaʻi, Dios mau,
ñisqa paʻa, kuyay hau.

[Verse 4]
Adán uē, Eva kaumaha,
Dios lohe, ñisqa aha,
kawsana kokoke, Qespichiq mai,
Paraíso hoʻi, suyay kai.

[Chorus]
iñiy lanakila, suyay ao,
Dios mau, kuyay hao,
kawsana ola, kawsay nui,
Qespichiq kokoke, hanan paʧa hui!

[Bridge]
wañuy pau, kawsay ola,
tuta holo, aʧana hola,
Dios mau, kuyay aloha,
kawsana kokoke, hanan paʧa ola.

[Outro]
iñiy mau... suyay ola...
