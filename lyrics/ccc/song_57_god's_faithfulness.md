Meneshinana 57: God's Faithfulness

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
ñisqa paʻa, Dios mau,
suyay ola, iñiy hau,
kawsana kokoke, Qespichiq mai,
kuyay nui, kawsay kai.

[Verse 2]
amaru hele mai, uʃpela lawe,
wañuy kokoke, Dios hawe,
kawsana hele mai, suyay ola,
iñiy paʻa, Qespichiq hola.

[Chorus]
ñisqa ola, Dios paʻa,
suyay mau, iñiy kā,
kawsana hele mai, kawsay ola,
Qespichiq nui, kuyay hola!

[Verse 3]
maʧay pōhaku, nina mehana,
yaku ola, kawsay banana,
ángel kiaʻi, Dios mau,
ñisqa paʻa, kuyay hau.

[Verse 4]
Adán uē, Eva kaumaha,
Dios lohe, ñisqa aha,
kawsana kokoke, Qespichiq mai,
Paraíso hoʻi, suyay kai.

[Chorus]
ñisqa ola, Dios paʻa,
suyay mau, iñiy kā,
kawsana hele mai, kawsay ola,
Qespichiq nui, kuyay hola!

[Bridge]
wañuy pau, kawsay ola,
tuta holo, aʧana hola,
Dios mau, kuyay aloha,
kawsana kokoke, hanan paʧa ola.

[Outro]
ñisqa mau... Dios paʻa...
