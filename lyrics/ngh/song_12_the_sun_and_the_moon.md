ǀXam 12: The Sun and the Moon

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
tsūb hele mai, ǀgamab holo,
ǃKhúb nui, ǀhāb polo,
ǃam paʻa, ǂnū mau,
ǃnubab ao, ǃhūb hau.

[Verse 2]
ǂnūb hele mai, ǂgāb lawe,
ǃgū kokoke, ǃKhúb hawe,
ǃnāb hele mai, ǀhāb ola,
ǃam paʻa, Sāob hola.

[Chorus]
ǀgamab e hele mai, tsūb pau,
ǃKhúb mālamalama, ǀhāb hau,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǃam ola,
Sāob nui, ǂom hola!

[Verse 3]
gao pōhaku, ǃaib mehana,
ǃam ola, ǂom banana,
ǃnau-amab kiaʻi, ǃKhúb mau,
ǀgamab paʻa, ǂnū hau.

[Verse 4]
ǃÁdam uē, ǃÉva kaumaha,
ǃKhúb lohe, ǀgamab aha,
ǃnāb kokoke, Sāob mai,
ǃNūs hoʻi, ǀhāb kai.

[Chorus]
ǀgamab e hele mai, tsūb pau,
ǃKhúb mālamalama, ǀhāb hau,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǃam ola,
Sāob nui, ǂom hola!

[Bridge]
ǃgū pau, ǂom ola,
tsūb holo, ǀgamab hola,
ǃKhúb mau, ǂnū aloha,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǃnubab ola.

[Outro]
ǀgamab mau... tsūb pau...
