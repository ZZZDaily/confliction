ǀXam 55: The Risen One

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Sāob ala, ǃgū pau,
ǃKhúb lanakila, ǀhāb hau,
ǂom ola mau, ǃam nui,
ǃnāb hele mai, ǂnū hui.

[Verse 2]
ǂnūb hele mai, ǂgāb lawe,
ǃgū kokoke, ǃKhúb hawe,
ǃnāb hele mai, ǀhāb ola,
ǃam paʻa, Sāob hola.

[Chorus]
Sāob ala, ǃgū hina,
ǃKhúb nui, ǂom ina,
ǀhāb ola, ǃam mau,
ǃnāb lanakila, ǃnubab hau!

[Verse 3]
gao pōhaku, ǃaib mehana,
ǃam ola, ǂom banana,
ǃnau-amab kiaʻi, ǃKhúb mau,
ǀgamab paʻa, ǂnū hau.

[Verse 4]
ǃÁdam uē, ǃÉva kaumaha,
ǃKhúb lohe, ǀgamab aha,
ǃnāb kokoke, Sāob mai,
ǃNūs hoʻi, ǀhāb kai.

[Chorus]
Sāob ala, ǃgū hina,
ǃKhúb nui, ǂom ina,
ǀhāb ola, ǃam mau,
ǃnāb lanakila, ǃnubab hau!

[Bridge]
ǃgū pau, ǂom ola,
tsūb holo, ǀgamab hola,
ǃKhúb mau, ǂnū aloha,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǃnubab ola.

[Outro]
Sāob ala... ǃgū pau...
