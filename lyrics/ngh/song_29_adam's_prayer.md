ǀXam 29: Adam's Prayer

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
ǃam paʻa, ǀhāb ola,
ǃKhúb nui, ǂnū hola,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǂom mau,
Sāob hele mai, ǃnubab hau.

[Verse 2]
ǂnūb hele mai, ǂgāb lawe,
ǃgū kokoke, ǃKhúb hawe,
ǃnāb hele mai, ǀhāb ola,
ǃam paʻa, Sāob hola.

[Chorus]
ǃam lanakila, ǀhāb ao,
ǃKhúb mau, ǂnū hao,
ǃnāb ola, ǂom nui,
Sāob kokoke, ǃnubab hui!

[Verse 3]
gao pōhaku, ǃaib mehana,
ǃam ola, ǂom banana,
ǃnau-amab kiaʻi, ǃKhúb mau,
ǀgamab paʻa, ǂnū hau.

[Verse 4]
ǃÁdam uē, ǃÉva kaumaha,
ǃKhúb lohe, ǀgamab aha,
ǃnāb kokoke, Sāob mai,
ǃNūs hoʻi, ǀhāb kai.

[Chorus]
ǃam lanakila, ǀhāb ao,
ǃKhúb mau, ǂnū hao,
ǃnāb ola, ǂom nui,
Sāob kokoke, ǃnubab hui!

[Bridge]
ǃgū pau, ǂom ola,
tsūb holo, ǀgamab hola,
ǃKhúb mau, ǂnū aloha,
ǃnāb kokoke, ǃnubab ola.

[Outro]
ǃam mau... ǀhāb ola...
