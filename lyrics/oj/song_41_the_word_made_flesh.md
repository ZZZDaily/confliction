ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 41: The Word Made Flesh

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid Zaagidiwin,
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid Debwewendamowin,
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid Zaagidiwin,
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Bimaadiziwin Bimaaji'iwewin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid Bimaadiziwin,
Bimaaji'iwewin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Gichi-Manidoo Debendaagozid... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
