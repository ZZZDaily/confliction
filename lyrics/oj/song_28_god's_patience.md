ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 28: God's Patience

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Zaagidiwin,
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Debwewendamowin,
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Zaagidiwin,
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Zhawenjigewin Debwewendamowin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Zhawenjigewin,
Debwewendamowin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
