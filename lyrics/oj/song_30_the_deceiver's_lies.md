ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 30: The Deceiver's Lies

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Waaséyaa, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin Gichi-Manidoo,
Waaséyaa Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Ginebig Maji-izhichigewin... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
