ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 60: Children of Adam

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Adam Bimaadiziwin Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Adam Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Adam Bimaadiziwin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Adam Bimaadiziwin Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Adam Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Adam Bimaadiziwin, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Apenimo, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Adam Bimaadiziwin Gichi-Manidoo,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Adam Bimaadiziwin... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
