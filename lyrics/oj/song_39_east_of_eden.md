ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 39: East of Eden

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Gitigaan Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gitigaan Aki Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Gitigaan Aki, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Gitigaan Aki Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gitigaan Aki Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Gitigaan Aki, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Gitigaan Aki Gichi-Manidoo,
Debwewendamowin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Gitigaan Aki... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
