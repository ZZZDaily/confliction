ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 12: The Sun and the Moon

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Waaséyaa Giizhig Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Waaséyaa Giizhig Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Waaséyaa Giizhig, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Waaséyaa Giizhig Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Waaséyaa Giizhig Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Waaséyaa Giizhig, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Waaséyaa Giizhig Gichi-Manidoo,
Bishigendaagoziwin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Waaséyaa Giizhig... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
