ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 63: From Glory to Glory

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-inendaagwad Giizhig, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo Gichi-inendaagwad,
Giizhig Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-Manidoo... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
