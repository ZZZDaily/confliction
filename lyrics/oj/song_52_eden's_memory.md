ᓂᑲᒧᐣ 52: Eden's Memory

[Intro]

[Verse 1]
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Zaagidiwin,
Apenimo Debwewendamowin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 2]
Aki Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Debwewendamowin.

[Chorus]
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Verse 3]
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Bimaadiziwin Zaagidiwin Apenimo,
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining Debwewendamowin,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Waaséyaa.

[Verse 4]
Bemaajiiwed Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin,
Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin Giizhig,
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining Zaagidiwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin Apenimo.

[Chorus]
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining, Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin!
Gichi-Manidoo Zaagidiwin, Gichi-inendaagwad Bimaadiziwin!
Giizhig Aki, Waaséyaa Dibiki-giizis,
Wawindamagewin Bimaaji'iwewin Apenimo!

[Bridge]
Nibi Mitig Bimaadiziwin,
Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin Gichi-inendaagwad,
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining Gichi-Manidoo,
Zaagidiwin Debwewendamowin Apenimo.

[Outro]
Gitigaan Minwaanigoziwining... Gichi-Manidoo Bishigendaagoziwin...
